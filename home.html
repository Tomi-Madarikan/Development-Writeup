DEVELOPMENT WRITEUP:

Application Goals and Value to A User:

This application is an online stationery shop. Users can choose from a range of twelve products including pencils, highlighters and pencil cases. The value to a user is that the app provides a one-stop shop for all of the stationery that you could fill your pencil case with.

Usability Principles Considered:

The app is easy to navigate because the filters and sort functions are all clearly displayed near the top of the page. Each product is displayed in a card that also lists the corresponding price, size and colour of the product. These cards are grouped in columns of 4 with uniform size and dimensions. Additionally, each card contains an ‘Add to Cart’ button and a “remove from Cart” button that enables adding/removing to and from the aggregator (the Shopping Cart).

The shopping cart is located at the bottom of the page and the total price of items in the cart clearly updates(aggregates) whenever a user adds/removes items to/from the aggregator. I believe that this layout fosters learnability and usability. I kept the colours simple and kept the contrast between text, images and background colour sufficiently high to foster readability.
